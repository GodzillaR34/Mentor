About Mentor

Mentor is an application which can help you find your career path. Mentor, as the name suggests, is also where many different mentors can help other in finding their career path. In other words, Mentor allows people who have issues deciding on their career path to get assistance from mentors and people who have also gone through similar situations.

Simple UI

With a simple and easy to use UI, it is almost imposible to get lost!

Why use Mentor?

Mentor has a down-to-earth User Interface. Our app is also very simple to handle, making it easy for everyone, knowledgeable, or with little knowledge, to use it without any issues.

The Goal


Our goal is to increase the rate of employment and reduce the inequalities faced by employees in their career.

The Inspiration


A person goes through a number of phases through their lives. During their childhood they are mostly exposed to rather vauge ambitions like an engineer, a doctor and a firefighter. But as they grow, there are so many types of engineers, so many roles, so many options to choose from. But they don't exactly know the responsibilities for each role. This can get overwhelming.

What's Next for Mentor?

We plan on integrating AI into our app to help match mentors with mentees better through the app. Currently, it is available on only 1 platform, Android. We plan on reaching it to iOS and possibly the web to allow more users to benefit and to help provide equality in work, increase employment to increase the economy and benefit humanity.
